Sample Translation Post - Tang Dynasty Poem

May 28, 2025 · Your Name
春眠不觉晓，
Chūn mián bù jué xiǎo,
[spring] [sleep] [not] [aware of] [dawn]
In spring I sleep unaware of dawn,
处处闻啼鸟。
Chù chù wén tí niǎo.
[everywhere] [hear] [cry] [birds]
Everywhere I hear birds singing.
夜来风雨声，
Yè lái fēng yǔ shēng,
[night] [come] [wind] [rain] [sound]
In the night came the sound of wind and rain,
花落知多少。
Huā luò zhī duō shǎo.
[flowers] [fall] [know] [how many]
How many flowers have fallen, I wonder?
